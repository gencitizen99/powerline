About this map

This is an unofficial map made by a community member. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced by Valley Link Transmission LLC, the Valley North project, PJM, or any government agency.

The proposed routes shown here are preliminary and subject to change. For the latest official information, visit the project website: vltransmission.com/valley-north
  • Proposed route, corridor & substations: map data published by the developer at vltransmission.com, downloaded July 6, 2026.
  • Legislative districts (optional overlay): WV Legislature 2021 redistricting boundaries via the WVU GIS Tech Center; contribution totals from public campaign-finance filings (cfrs.wvsos.gov) — see the Developer donations page.
  • Address search: OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoder (© OpenStreetMap contributors).
  • Property lines & owner information: streamed live from the West Virginia Property Viewer (mapwv.gov), maintained by the WV GIS Technical Center from county assessor records. Shown as-is and may contain errors — verify important details with your county assessor.

This map is for community information only and is not legal or surveying advice.
