Valley North: opposition & legislator scoreboard

Unofficial community tracker  •  All counties & parcels  •  Developer donations  •  About
What this tracks: (1) formal action by county governments on the proposed Valley North 765 kV line, and (2) every West Virginia state legislator who represents an impacted county, with their position as it becomes known. County resolutions and legislator statements aren’t binding on the PSC, but they shape the public record and the politics. Maintained by a community member — send corrections or updates with a link to the article, minutes, or statement.
